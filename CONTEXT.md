# Ubiquitous Language — Matura Prep

## Core Domain Terms

**Target**
The desired final matura score expressed as a percentage (0–100%). All planning and progress evaluation is measured against this value.

**Time Horizon**
The number of months remaining until the matura exam date. Used to determine urgency and pacing of the preparation plan.

**Possible Commitment**
The number of hours per week a student realistically can dedicate to learning. Accounts for school, rest, and other obligations — not an ideal maximum, but a sustainable estimate.
